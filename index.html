Formerly called: OpticHaus

Scrum Master

January 2018 - December 2018

  • A virtual reality application designed to aid architecture and interior design students to take into consideration special needs users - such as wheelchair users and individuals with different vision impairments. Human Eyes Design helps users experience their 3D Computer Aided Design models in virtual reality while experiencing their designs through the shoes of wheelchair users, individuals with cataracts, glaucoma, macular degeneration, and diabetic retinopathy. The application is designed to not only educate future architects and interior designers, but also aid in building empathy towards the needs of special needs user.
