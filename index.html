EDUCATION

I currently study at the University of Florida as a Computer Science major.

Originally, I began college as a Computer Engineering major due to my previous experience as building computers. At UF, Computer Engineering majors and Computer Science majors share a lot of the core classes and I realized that I enjoyed developing software more and changed to Computer Science in my second semester. I've been a part of several students organization at UF's Computer and Information Science and Engineering - the UF Association for Computing Machinery and UF Hackathoners. My time at UF has helped me realize my passion for software development and how to use my passion to build useful, valuable software.

I will be graduating in December 2019.
